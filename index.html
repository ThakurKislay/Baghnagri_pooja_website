About Us

Pooja Samiti is a community organization dedicated to promoting spiritual growth and fostering religious harmony within our community.

Founded in [year], our organization has been serving the needs of [location] residents for [number] years. We believe in the power of collective prayer and the importance of coming together as a community to celebrate our faith and traditions.
